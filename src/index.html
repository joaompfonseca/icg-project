Information

  • Goal
    • Colonize all the planets in the level.
  • Spaceships
    • Colonized planets produce spaceships for the owner.
    • Spaceships can be sent between planets.
    • Spaceships destroy enemy's spaceships in a 1:1 ratio.
  • Colonization
    • Colonize a planet by sending spaceships to it.
    • Process is quicker the more spaceships there are on the planet.
    • Removing all spaceships during colonization interrupts the process.
  • Controls
    • Click and drag to rotate the camera.
    • Scroll to zoom in and out.
    • Click a planet with owned spaceships to select it.
      • Left click another planet to send half of the spaceships.
      • Right click another planet to send one spaceship.
